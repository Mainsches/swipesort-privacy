2. General Information

Protecting your personal data is important to us. This privacy policy explains what data may be collected when using the “SwipeSort” app and how it is used.

3. Local Data Processing

The “SwipeSort” app processes photos exclusively locally on your device.

Users remain in full control of their photos at all times.

No photos are uploaded, stored, or processed on external servers.

3a. Duplicate Finder Feature

The app includes a premium feature called “Duplicate Finder” that analyzes photos stored on your device to detect exact duplicate files.

This analysis is performed entirely locally on the device using file-based comparison methods.

No photos are uploaded, transmitted, or stored externally during this process.

The feature only identifies exact duplicate files and allows the user to manually review and delete selected copies.

4. Data Collection

This app may process the following types of data:

  • anonymous usage data (e.g., app interactions, screen views, session duration)
  • technical data (e.g., device type, operating system, app version)
  • advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • crash and performance data

5. Analytics (Firebase)

This app uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC.

Firebase may process information such as:

  • app usage behavior
  • device information
  • interaction events

Data is processed only with your consent and used to improve the app.

These services may collect and process data independently in accordance with their own privacy policies.

More information:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

6. Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob, a service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements.

AdMob may use device information and an advertising ID to display personalized or non-personalized ads.

Depending on your consent, ads may be personalized. Without consent, only non-personalized ads will be shown.

These services may collect and process data independently in accordance with their own privacy policies.

More information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Consent and Control

When using the app for the first time, you will be asked to consent to analytics and advertising.

You can change your preferences at any time in the app settings.

8. Purpose of Processing

Data is used to:

  • improve app performance
  • identify and fix errors
  • optimize user experience

9. Legal Basis

Processing is based on your consent and our legitimate interest in improving and securely operating the app.

10. Data Sharing

Data may be shared with third-party providers (Google Firebase and Google AdMob) as described above.

11. International Data Transfers

The services used may transfer data to countries outside Switzerland or the EU, including the United States.

12. Data Retention

Data is stored only as long as necessary for the purposes described.

13. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • request access to your data
  • request correction or deletion
  • withdraw consent at any time
  • object to processing

14. Data Security

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are used to protect your data.
